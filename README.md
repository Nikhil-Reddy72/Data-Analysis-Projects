📊 Electronic Sales Analysis

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing electronic sales transaction data to identify key patterns in revenue, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance.
The objective is to extract actionable insights that can support data-driven decision-making in sales strategy, pricing, and inventory management.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Python
SQL
Power BI
Pandas
NumPy
Seaborn
Matplotlib


📈 Key Metrics

Total Revenue: $1.2 Billion
Total Profit: $461 Million
Profit Margin: 37%
Total Orders: 64,000


🔍 Key Insights

California contributes approximately 19.5% of total revenue, making it the top-performing region
The Wholesale channel generates the highest share of revenue among all sales channels
Certain products yield higher profit margins despite lower sales volume, indicating opportunities for strategic promotion
Revenue distribution is uneven across regions, highlighting potential areas for market expansion

💼 Business Impact

This analysis provides valuable insights that help businesses:

Identify high-performing and high-margin products
Optimize pricing strategies for better profitability
Understand regional sales trends and customer behavior
Improve inventory planning and demand forecasting
Enhance channel-based marketing strategies
🚀 Conclusion

The project demonstrates how data analysis and visualization can transform raw sales data into meaningful insights, enabling businesses to make informed decisions and improve overall performance.
